About the Program

PyCoders is a completely free 7 months-long IT bootcamp in FENYX where we aim to upskill talented newcomers and help them start a new career in IT -specifically in data and analytics. With a modern tech stack and ever-growing network, we thrive to help newcomers establish their new lives in their new homes.

A New Career in IT

Data & Analytics is arguably the fastest growing field in IT. With the abundance of data and emergence of new technologies to cultivate these data, new job openings emerge every single day. With a solid education, you can land jobs as junior data analyst, data engineer, or data scientist in Data & Analytics field.

Free Bootcamp Education

PyCoders is completely free. It is a social initiative backed by Fenyx IT Academy which is a nonprofit organization and the aim is the upskilling of talented newcomers. Our classes are taught by voluntary mentors.

Career-Coaching & Soft Skills

We know it takes solid soft-skills as well as technical ability to land a job. We curate a special module where we provide training for expressing yourself in the best way possible during the interviews, preparing for technical rounds, showcasing your portfolio, and establishing a solid online presence in professional networking platforms.
