Course Details

  • Training will last for 7 months.
  • We will have two classes each weekend.
  • Some classes will be held in English and some in Turkish.
  • Classes will be held in Amsterdam (if allowed by Covid-19 health protocols). Otherwise, it will be online.
  • You'll need around 30 to 40 hours every week for studies and assignments.
  • You'll get a certification when you have successfully graduated.



We expect you to:

  • Have English communication skills at least at an intermediate level.
  • Be willing to study 30-40 hours a week.
  • Be located in the Netherlands.
  • Have a refugee background.
  • Have the motivation to pursue internships and/or jobs upon graduation.

Note: In your motivation letter, please tell us briefly about why you want to become a trainee in our course and about your future plans.
